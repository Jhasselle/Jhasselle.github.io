Third-card rules

Cards count face value; 10s and face cards count 0; only the last digit of the total matters.

Naturals: either hand showing 8 or 9 on the first two cards — both stand.

Player hits on 0–5, stands on 6–7.

Banker hits on 0–2 no matter what.

3hit — but don't hit on 8
4hit on 2–7

7always stand

No third card on the Player hand? Banker hits 0–5, stands 6–7.
